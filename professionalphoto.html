Syeda and Mickey's Wedding

Kristi shot a wedding held in Homestead, Florida on January 18th, 2014.

The young flower girl warily strolled down the asile, constantly glancing back at her mother, who was just a few steps behind. Following the ceremony, the flower girl decided she still wanted to have all of her flowers so she picked up all of the petals she had littered across the floor minutes before.

Syeda pensively gazes into the mirror ahead while her friend and relative adorns her with her veil minutes before she is due to walk down the asile.

Quickly arranging her veil, Syeda begins displaying a look of excitement mixed with twinge of apprehension.

Mickey and Syeda met each other when Syeda took Mickey's boxing class. Fittingly, the newly married couple ended the night in a fake boxing match.
