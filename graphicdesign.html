Experience

Kristi is proficient with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, utilizing both to create projects, edit photos and to create other media content.

Static Website Design

For Introduction to Multimedia Design at the University of Miami, Kristi was required to create a static website design for a topic of her choice. Kristi created a fictional group called "Festival Junkies" who traveled to different music festivals across the United States and documented their thoughts, tips and suggestions for readers on their website.

Festival Junkies website homepage mockup. The menu will have dropdown tabs including the page subsections when hovered over.

Movie Poster Project

Also in the Introduction to Multimedia Design class at the University of Miami, Kristi needed to create two movie posters for a movie of her choice. She could only utilize Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Kristi Townsend's Juno Movie Poster One, made for her Multimedia Design class at the University of Miami. This poster needed to include images, which Kristi decided to utilize photos of the two main characters in the movie. She drew iconic symbols from the movie using the pen tool on Adobe Illustrator to create a border lining the perimeters of the poster.

Kristi Townsend's Juno movie poster two, also made for her Multimedia Design class at the University of Miami. This poster could only be made with vector graphics, which Kristi drew all of them using the pen tool on Adobe Illustrator.
